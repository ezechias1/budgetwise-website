Everything you need to know about BudgetWise

BudgetWise is a free budgeting and financial tracking app that helps you manage your money. You can track expenses, set savings goals, scan receipts, get smart financial advice, and much more. It works on any device with a web browser.

Yes! The core Personal budgeting features are completely free with no credit card required. This includes expense tracking, savings goals, charts, budget limits, receipt scanning, CSV/PDF export, recurring expenses, smart advice, and more. The Pro plan (R79/month or R659/year) unlocks Business and Family modes.

Personal (free) - Track your everyday expenses, set savings goals, and manage your personal budget.
Business (Pro) - Manage business expenses, create invoices, track clients, and view profit & loss reports.
Family (Pro) - Manage your household budget with family members, allowances, chores & rewards, and shared goals.

Yes! BudgetWise is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on any device. You can install it on your phone's home screen for a native app-like experience. It works on Android, iOS, and desktop browsers.

On your dashboard, tap the "Scan" button next to "Add Expense". Take a photo of your receipt or upload an image, and BudgetWise will automatically extract the amount using OCR (optical character recognition) powered by Tesseract.js. You can then confirm and categorize the expense.

Absolutely. Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We use SSL/TLS encryption, row-level security on all database tables, and secure authentication. We never store your bank login credentials. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Bank linking is currently in beta and available for select regions. We use secure third-party providers (Plaid, Salt Edge) to connect to your bank - we never see or store your bank login details. This feature is being expanded and will be available to more users soon.
