Coming soon

Introducing BudgetWise Junior

The easiest way to teach your kids the value of money. Built for ages 10–13, inside the BudgetWise app you already use.

Chores that pay

Assign chores with rand rewards you choose. Your kid ticks them off, you approve, and the app tracks what you owe them.

Daily money lessons

Short, age-appropriate missions that teach saving, spending, and giving. Set a reward per lesson — your call.

Save / Spend / Give jars

Your kid picks how to split their money. Every time you settle up, the split happens automatically — the habit forms itself.

You approve everything

Kids can't spend, edit, or assign. You approve chores, set lesson rewards, and mark payments as paid — you stay in control.

A real IOU ledger

No fake money. The app shows exactly what you owe each kid — "You owe Sarah R85" — and reminds you to settle up.

One app for every kid

Add as many children as you want. Each gets their own avatar, PIN, savings goal, and mission progress — no sibling overlap.

Included in Pro — no separate subscription. Launches as part of a BudgetWise update coming soon.
